What's working against you

Know it.
Avoid it.

Your visceral fat 13.0 is directly driven by certain foods. Even cutting one category makes a real difference. Start with Critical.
🚨

Critical — Feeds Visceral Fat Directly

Critical
🥤
Sugary drinks — soda, juice, energy drinks
Liquid sugar spikes insulin fast, goes straight to visceral fat. Worst offender for your score.
Water, sparkling water, unsweetened tea or coffee
🍟
Fried food — fries, fried chicken, samosas
Trans & saturated fats store 3x more likely as visceral fat vs other fat types.
Grilled, baked, or air-fried versions
🍰
Baked goods — cakes, cookies, pastries
Sugar + saturated fat combined is the worst combo for visceral fat.
Dates, fruit, or a small square of dark chocolate
⚠️

High Impact — Limit These

High
🍞
White bread, white rice, white pasta
Refined carbs digest like sugar — rapid blood sugar spike, fat storage.
Brown rice, whole wheat, or just smaller portions
🥩
Fatty processed meats — sausage, bacon
High saturated fat + sodium linked directly to visceral fat growth.
Chicken breast, turkey, eggs, tuna
🍺
Alcohol
Body burns alcohol before fat — fat loss completely stops while alcohol is in your system.
Sparkling water with lemon at social events
📌

Moderate — Watch Portions

Moderate
🍦
Ice cream, candy, chocolate bars
High-fructose corn syrup processed directly into belly fat by the liver.
70%+ dark chocolate, 1–2 squares max
📦
Frozen / packaged meals
High sodium causes water retention, hidden sugars add up fast.
Eggs + anything is better than most packaged meals
Activities to avoid
🛋️
Sitting 2+ hours without moving
Shuts off the enzyme that burns fat. Stand up every 45–60 min.
🌙
Eating right before sleeping
Carbs and sugar eaten late get stored as fat. Stop eating 2 hours before bed.
😴
Less than 6 hours sleep
Raises hunger hormones, lowers fullness hormones. Body holds fat harder when sleep-deprived.
🏃
Running, jumping, burpees
High-impact risks real joint injury at your current weight. Stick to these exercises until you drop 15–20kg.
Smart moves

Work smarter.
Not harder.

🥇 Biggest single change: Replace sugary drinks with water. Just this, nothing else, reduces visceral fat over 3 months. A can of soda is ~150 kcal — that's 7% of your entire daily burn just sitting there.
💧 Drink more water. Your body water is 47.2% — should be 55–65%. Low hydration slows metabolism, increases hunger, and makes joints hurt more. Aim for 2.5–3 litres a day.
🚶 Walk after eating. Even 10 minutes blunts the blood sugar spike from that meal — directly reducing how much gets stored as fat.
🍽️ Eat protein first. When you sit down to eat, start with the meat, eggs, or legumes. Fills you faster so you naturally eat fewer carbs after.
⏰ Stop eating 2 hours before bed. Do the workout, then stop eating. Your body fasts overnight and burns fat while you sleep.
💪 Your secret weapon: Your 65.6kg muscle mass burns ~900–1000 kcal/day just existing — nearly half your BMR. Most people your size have far less. The workout keeps this engine alive.
📈 Realistic target: With nightly workouts and cutting Critical foods, expect 1–2kg loss per month. Visceral fat drops faster than total weight — your joints will feel it within the first month.
🔁 Consistency beats intensity. 7 minutes every night beats 1 hour once a week. Show up, press Start, done.
